MISSION:
Asian American Youth Leadership (AAYL) is a non-profit (501c3) organization dedicated to cultivate young leaders within the Asian American community by encouraging youth activity in recreational and educational projects and enriching their experiences as community memebers. Our focus is to spreahead the drive to establish a youth leadership that can serve our youth & families and surrounding citizens in our community.

Join Us

Why is leadership important?

Asian Americans have historically been underrepresented in leadership
positions in various fields such as politics, business, and media.
This lack of leadership causes the under representation of Asian
American culture.

How is leadership defind?

Leadership is the act of guiding and influencing individuals or organizations towards a common goal. It involves effective communication, decision-making, motivation, and empowerment. A good leader possesses the necessary skills, knowledge, and traits to inspire and guide their team towards success.

Why join AAYL?

AAYL provides various leadership development programs, mentorship opportunities, and community service projects that can help you develop your leadership skills and gain valuable experience. Your experience here at AAYL will carry you and your community further.
