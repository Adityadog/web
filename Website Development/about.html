About Us

MISSION:
Asian American Youth Leadership (AAYL) is a non-profit (501c3) organization dedicated to cultivate young leaders within the Asian American community by encouraging youth activity in recreational and educational projects and enriching their experiences as community memebers. Our focus is to spreahead the drive to establish a youth leadership that can serve our youth & families and surrounding citizens in our community.

AAYL aims to provide youths the opportunity to gain leadership experiences. These leadership skills wil provide the future generation with the necessary communication and awareness skills to better the community and the world.
